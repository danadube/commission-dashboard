✨ Key Improvements

  • Reduced from 9+ colors to 6 semantic colors
  • Consistent saturation (65-80%) creates visual harmony
  • Gradients only where meaningful (brand identity, depth)
  • Solid colors for clarity and instant recognition
  • HSB-based hover states (darker = more saturated)
  • Fixed dark mode with proper shades instead of opacity
  • All colors meet WCAG AA accessibility standards
  • Clear semantic meaning for each color
